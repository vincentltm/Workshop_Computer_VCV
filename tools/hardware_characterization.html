⚡ Step 1 — Set Output Level (20 Vpp Reference)

Connect PC headphone out → Workshop STEREO IN. Patch STEREO IN OUT to your oscilloscope. Click Start Cal Tone and adjust your PC volume + STEREO IN gain trim until the scope reads exactly 20 V peak-to-peak (−10 V to +10 V). This sets 0 dBFS = 10 V peak across all measurements.

🎛 Step 2 — Measure Interface Input Level

Route STEREO IN OUT into your interface input (reduce interface gain to avoid clipping). Click Measure. The tool records noise floor silently, then plays the cal tone and captures its peak — computing a voltage scale factor so all results are in real Volts.
