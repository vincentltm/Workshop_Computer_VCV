Quick Tips

  • Tap Down: Cycle parameter pages.
  • Hold Down (2s): Preset Menu. (X Left = Load, X Right = Save).
  • Tap Up: Cycle synthesis models.
  • Hold Up (2s): Sequencer Settings. (Main: Dens, X: BPM, Y: Ent).
  • Always Active: The Generative Engine runs at all times. Patch CV1/Pulse1 back to inputs to self-play.
